About me

I am a Petroleum Engineer graduated from Universidad de Oriente in Venezuela, with 5 years of experience in the Oil & Gas industry. Now I am taking a Certificate in Computer Programming at BYU-Idaho and a Full Stack developer course at Toti Diversidade to expand my background and increase the working opportunities.

I am married to José Bastidas since 2016, and we have a beautiful and sweet baby called Sunimar Antonella, who is 3 years old.

I love to travel, doing outdoor activities, learning about new cultures, and spend time with family and friends.

My native language is the Spanish. I am fluent in English, and I'm learning Portuguese.

Experience

Feb 2020- Apr 2020

Reservoir Engineer / Schlumberger Brazil

I was part of the Brazil Technology Integration Center (BTiC) whose main purpose is to promote the integration of geosciences and engineering to improve hydrocarbon production and recovery from the complex deep-water reservoirs and pre-salt carbonates offshore in Brazil.

Reviewing and validating some data to be used in the sensitivity analysis of 3D core simulations.

Qc and developing the first steps of a reservoir model to be used in the project: integration modeling of formation damage, thermal effects, and induced fractures during water injection in pre-salt carbonates.

Sep 2013- May 2016

Reservoir Engineer / Schlumberger Venezuela

Consultant in several projects, where assisted in the generation of dynamic reservoir models for creating Field Development plans - one of them involved the asphaltene precipitation modeling.

Support staff (on-site & on-line) to clients from PDVSA and International Oil Companies in Venezuela, Trinidad & Tobago. I mainly supported the following reservoir simulators/software: ECLIPSE (Black Oil, Compositional, Thermal, PVTi), INTERSECT, and Petrel Reservoir Engineering.

Instructor for courses related to the Schlumberger Reservoir Engineering applications such as Petrel Reservoir Engineering, Petrel Fundamentals, and ECLIPSE Suite.

Feb 2020- Apr 2020

Petroleum Engineer / Miranda Consultores Integrados

Collecting, organizing and validating well data (wellheads, well tops, deviations, completions, logs, mechanic history, production history, etc) and rock & fluid data (core analysis, PVTs, etc) needed to build a 3D Reservoir Simulation Model in Petrel.

Making reports about the mechanical history of wells (drilling, original completion, workover, repair, service, etc.

Performing conventional reservoir evaluations (physical parameters, fluid properties, production, DCA, volumetric estimation, material balance, reserves).

Verifying and digitizing well logs.
